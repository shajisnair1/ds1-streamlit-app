Note 1. FLUT (WT/FL) is required for drill pipe in lieu of EMI when either the normal wall thickness is greater than 0.500" or verification of wall thickness along the entire tube length is critical.
Note 2. For Category 5 Inspection, Blacklight Connection Inspection for fatigue crack in drill pipe tool joints is relatively expensive when performed on large lots of drill pipe, and fatigue failures in drill pipe tool joints are rare. Users may consider omitting Blacklight Connection Inspection of drill pipe tool joints from the Category 5 inspection program unless the joint fatigue cracks have occurred. Other Category 5 inspections are recommended. Blacklight Connection Inspection is still required on final components for Category 2 and higher.
Note 3. For ferromagnetic components, Wet Viable Contrast Inspection may be substituted for MPI Slip/Upseel Area Inspection.
