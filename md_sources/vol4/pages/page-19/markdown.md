## 1.8 Waivers

The vendor shall have a process in place to document all waivers and/or exceptions to the requirements of this standard when DS-1 Volume 4 is specified by the customer. Documented waivers might include:

- Deviations from the manufacturer's maintenance requirements or recommended practices. These shall be documented as part of the appropriate maintenance procedures (inspection, assembly, or testing).
