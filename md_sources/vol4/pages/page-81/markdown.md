If the Visual Connection Inspection (section 7.14) will not be performed, steps 7.14.4, 7.14.5.6, 7.14.5.9, and 7.14.5.10, shall be added to this procedure.

a. Box Outside Diameter (OD). The OD of the tool joint box shall be measured 3/8 inch ±1/8 inch from the shoulder. At least two measurements shall be taken spaced at intervals of 90 degrees ±10 degrees. Box OD shall meet the requirements in Table 7.4, 7.29, or 7.30, as applicable. If a conflict arises, the manufacturer’s criteria shall apply.

b. Pin Inside Diameter (ID). The pin ID shall be measured under the last thread nearest the shoulder (±1/4 inch) and shall meet the requirements of Table 7.4, 7.29, or 7.30, as applicable. If a conflict arises, the manufacturer’s criteria shall apply.

c. Box Shoulder Width. The box shoulder width shall be measured by placing the straightedge longitudinally along the tool joint, extending past the shoulder surface, and then measuring the shoulder thickness from this extension to the counterbore (excluding any ID bevel). The shoulder width shall be measured at its point of minimum thickness. Any reading that does not meet the minimum shoulder width requirement in Table 7.4, 7.29, or 7.30, as applicable, shall cause the tool joint to be rejected. If a conflict arises, the manufacturer’s criteria shall apply.

d. Tong Space. Box and pin tong space (excluding the OD bevel) shall meet the requirements of Table 7.4, 7.29, or 7.30, as applicable. If a conflict arises, the manufacturer’s criteria shall apply. Tong space measurements on hardfaced components shall be made from the bevel to the edge of the hardfacing.
