## 5.9 Acceptance Criteria for Elevator Grooves on Drill Collars

The inspection of drill collar elevator grooves has been covered in past editions of DS-1 Volume 3, but in the Fifth Edition these inspections have been renewed. Recent industry failures have highlighted to the API committees that there was a weakness in available drill collar elevator sizes and their ability to safely lift collars in many cases. The API committees have decided to no longer support square-shouldered drill collar elevators or traditional square-shouldered drill collar elevator grooves. The DS-1 sponsor group agrees and has followed this pattern.
